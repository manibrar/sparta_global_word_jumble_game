Word Jumble




Welcome to Word jumble!

The aim of the game is to guess the jumbled word using all available letters before the time runs out. To make a guess click the letters in the panels to enter the word into guess preview. You can press the shuffle button to rearrange the letters and reset to start again. If you guess the word correctly you will advance to the next level, when your go is over you can enter your name and challenge your friends to beat your score.
